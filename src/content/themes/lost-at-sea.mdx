Living on an island in the Atlantic, we know the sea holds secrets. This month, we're diving into stories where the ocean is more than just a backdropâ€”it's a force of nature, a source of mystery, and sometimes, a hungry void that swallows ships whole.

## What fits this theme?

- Shipwreck survival stories
- Underwater civilizations (mermaids, Atlantis, etc.)
- Nautical horror and deep-sea terrors
- Stories about mysterious disappearances at sea
- Submarine or underwater base sci-fi
- Pirate adventures with fantastical elements
- Climate fiction with rising seas

## The Bermuda Triangle connection

We couldn't resist! While we're not requiring Triangle-specific stories (they're surprisingly rare in fiction), any tale of unexplained oceanic phenomena definitely qualifies. If you find a good one, we want to hear about it!

## Content notes

Many ocean stories involve drowning, isolation, and claustrophobic situations. If that's not your jam, look for the more adventurous, swashbuckling end of the spectrum.
