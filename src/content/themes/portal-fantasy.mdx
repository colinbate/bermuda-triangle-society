## What we're looking for

- The *transition* between worlds matters
- The new world should feel meaningfully different
- Bonus points for examining what happens when (if?) characters return

## Not quite a fit

- Secondary world fantasy where we just *start* in another world (like Lord of the Rings)
- Time travel alone doesn't count (unless you're traveling to a whole different reality)
- Parallel universe stories *might* work if there's a clear crossing moment
