## Next Time

We decided on our next theme: Morally Gray.

Again I'm leaving it a bit vague and up to interpretation. That said, the idea is books with characters that:

- Do bad things for understandable reasons
- Justify questionable actions (to other characters or the readers)
- Sit somewhere between hero and villain

Typically this isn't a case of "one bad decision", but rather a character with moral flexibility.

There is no "wrong" answer, if you think the book fits, it probably does. Enjoy the murkiness. :)
