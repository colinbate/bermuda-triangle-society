## On To The Next

It was a bit of a journey, but I think we landed on our next theme: The Classics.

There are a great many "classic" sci-fi and fantasy novels out there. Some are classic for a reason, others maybe haven't aged as well. There are even modern classics, ones that aren't as old, but whether through popularity or cult status find themselves in a place of reverence. You probably have one or two on your TBR list already.

However you define it, the challenge this month is to read something classic. I will provide a list of some titles to get you started, but pretty much anything from these authors would qualify. You can email me or the group if you want more options.

### Sample of Classic Authors

- Phillip K. Dick
- Octavia E. Butler
- H.G. Wells
- J.R.R. Tolkien
- Isaac Asimov
- Arthur C. Clarke
- Ray Bradbury
- Samuel R. Delany
- Mary Shelley
